A good way to approach this is to start with the specific problems your team has today, then design a lightweight tool around those needs instead of trying to build a full Jira/Asana replacement right away.

A simple MVP could include:
- Project dashboard with status, owners, deadlines, and priorities
- Task tracking with assignees, due dates, and progress
- Team timeline or roadmap view
- Notes, decisions, and blockers for each project
- Notifications or reminders for overdue work
- Basic reporting so managers can quickly see what’s on track or at risk

If you want, I’d suggest building it in phases:
1. Define the workflow your team already uses
2. List the biggest pain points
3. Pick the 3–5 most important features for version 1
4. Design a simple data model
5. Build a small prototype and test it with the team

A strong first question is: **what is currently breaking down most often — visibility, ownership, deadlines, communication, or prioritization?**
